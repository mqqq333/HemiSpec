## Study question

Do reconstruction-derived hemispheric specificity features capture structural information related to handedness beyond conventional gray-matter-volume asymmetry indices?

## Analyses in the current working scope

- Reconstruction plausibility of generated contralateral maps.
- Preservation of individual-specific information.
- Test-retest reliability of ANS/RNS maps.
- Hemisphere identity classification from ROI-level ANS/RNS features.
- Handedness classification against a conventional GMV asymmetry-index baseline.
- Feature localization and overlap between hemisphere-identity and handedness-related profiles.

## Public result boundary

Until the manuscript or preprint is public, this page should frame handedness as a methodological test case. Detailed result comparisons, model-performance claims, tables, and manuscript-specific figures should remain out of the public homepage.
